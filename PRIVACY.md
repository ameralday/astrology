# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 20, 2026

This policy describes how the Astrology app ("the app") handles your information. The short
version: your birth details stay on your device, our backend never stores anything, and we don't
share or sell your data to anyone.

## Information you provide

When you set up your profile, the app asks for:

- Your name (optional)
- Your birth date, time, and place of birth

This information is used only to calculate your Vedic astrological chart and daily readings.

## How your information is stored

Your birth profile and calculated chart are stored **only on your device**, using your phone's
local app storage. We do not operate a database, and this information is never uploaded to or
stored on our servers. If you uninstall the app, or use the "Clear my saved birth data" option
inside the app, this information is permanently deleted.

## How your information is used

To calculate your chart and daily readings, your birth date, time, and location are sent to our
backend server at the moment you request them. The backend performs the astronomical calculation
and returns the result — it does not log, save, or retain this information in any form. Each
request is processed independently and then discarded.

Searching for your birth place happens entirely on your device, using a location dataset bundled
with the app. This does not involve any network request or third party — your search terms are
never sent anywhere.

## Third parties

The app does not currently use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services, and
does not share your information with any third party. If that changes in the future (for example,
if ad-supported features are added), this policy will be updated in advance to disclose exactly
what's collected and by whom, before any such feature is turned on.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children and is not knowingly used to collect information from
children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your region).

## Your choices

You can delete your stored birth data at any time using "Clear my saved birth data" in the app,
or by uninstalling the app entirely. Because nothing is stored on our servers, there is no
separate account or server-side data to request deletion of.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app changes. Material changes will be reflected here with an
updated effective date.
